How To Send Money

We pride ourselves on keeping things simple and making your life easier. Here is how you can make international money transfers online in a few easy steps.

Create Your Payment Instruction

       First you create an electronic payment instruction on our website. You just tell us how much money you are transferring overseas and where you want us to send it to. The rates of exchange you see on our currency calculator are the exact exchange rates your cash will be transferred at.

Transfer Funds To NME

       Once you have created your payment instruction you then need to wire the payment total to the local NME bank account where you are. This is a domestic online bank to bank transfer and you will not be charged for this by your bank.

Payment Made To Recipient

       Once we have received your cleared deposit, into our local account, we will perform the online currency exchange for you and then transfer the money overseas to your beneficiary. In most cases the electronic funds transfer is completed the next working day after funds have been received.





How To Receive Money

You can collect your money in different ways:
With our agents in associates in major parts of cities, you will be able to receive money faster easier and with better convenience

Cash

       Collect your cash from our nearest store. Beneficiary is required to bring their id to collect the money.

Bank Deposit

       Your money is deposited into the bank. You are required to provide the details of the bank that you will deposit your money.

NetBank Transfer

       We will transfer your money into your bank account as provided by you. Incase absence of netbank in some countries the alternative way would be bank deposit.
